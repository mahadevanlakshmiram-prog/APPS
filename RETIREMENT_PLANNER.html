📋 How to use:

  • Enter your current age and retirement age (when you plan to stop working)
  • Add your current savings (nest egg), monthly income and monthly expenses
  • Set expected inflation (6% is typical for India) and expected investment returns:
    💡 Understanding Investment Returns:
    • Risk-free returns: RBI bond after-tax returns of 5.6% (8% × 0.7) is taken as a proxy for risk-free returns.
    • Risk capital returns: Long-term NIFTY Index returns of 11% is assumed for equity/risk capital.
    • Example: If your portfolio comprises 50% risk-free and 50% risky assets, your portfolio returns would be estimated at 8.3% average (0.5 × 5.6% + 0.5 × 11%).
    • You can estimate returns for any other combination of risk-free and risky assets based on your asset allocation.
  • Choose life expectancy (default 100 years) to see if your corpus lasts your lifetime
  • Click "Calculate" to see projections with your current income growth rate, or "Solve" to find the exact rate needed
